FAQ
When do limits reset?
Monthly. Your credit balance resets on renewal.
What counts as a minute?
Credits are billed by output duration for generation (rounded up). Inputs like clone/design have fixed credit costs.
Where do invoices live?
Account → Billing. We'll also link to the Stripe portal once billing is enabled.
About Vocra

Built for Audio
Creators.

Vocra is a precision voice tool for creators, studios, and developers who need high-fidelity speech synthesis without the overhead of managing models.

We're obsessed with audio quality and developer ergonomics. Every feature is built around the principle that generating speech should be as frictionless as typing text.
